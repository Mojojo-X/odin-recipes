Baked Tofu

Summarized version of my favorite baked tofu recipe, goes great in an air fryer.

Source: Baked Tofu by Megan Gilmore

  • 1 block extra-firm tofu
  • 1 Tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 Tbsp of starch
  1. Drain, press, slice tofu in half height-wise
  2. Pat dry with paper towel and cut into cubes
  3. Toss tofu in oil, mix seasoning then stir it into the tofu
  4. Add starch until it's fully incorporated
  5. spread evenly onto baking sheet or air fry basket, then bake/air-fry at 400F for 20 - 25 minutes
